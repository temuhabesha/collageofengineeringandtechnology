civil Engineering

The civil engineering department at our institution was established in September 2015 E.C. with a vision to nurture future professionals in the field of civil engineering. Now, we have two staff members who have master’s degrees, and we have engaged in delivering common courses and establishing departments like laboratories, workshops, and computer labs.

The department aims to provide a comprehensive and innovative academic program that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience necessary to address the complex challenges facing the built environment.

The establishment of the civil engineering department reflects our institution’s dedication to advancing knowledge and expertise in the field of civil engineering, contributing to the sustainable development of infrastructure, and promoting innovation in construction practices. Through cutting-edge research, state-of-the-art facilities, and a diverse and inclusive learning environment, the department aims to be a hub of excellence in civil engineering education and research, catalyzing positive change in society.

As we embark on this journey of academic excellence and professional growth, the civil engineering department is poised to make significant contributions to the advancement of civil engineering knowledge, practice, and impact, shaping the future of infrastructure development and urban planning in our region and beyond.
